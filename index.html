What Noah Teaches Us

Four simple things we can learn from Noah.

Obedience (Genesis 6:22)

Doing what God says, even when it is hard.

Faith (Hebrews 11:7)

Trusting God before you see the final result.

Courage (Genesis 7:1)

Standing up for what is right, even if you are alone.

Hope (Genesis 9:13)

Believing that God always keeps His promises.

Noah's Journey

Read the story of Noah, the man who built the Ark.

HEBREWS 11:7 PROGRESSION

1
Step 01 - Genesis 6:13

God spoke to Noah.

God told Noah His plan to save the world.

2
Step 02 - Genesis 6:14-22

Noah built the Ark.

Noah built the Ark even though it had never rained.

3
Step 03 - Genesis 7:7-9

Animals entered the Ark.

God sent animals of every kind into the Ark.

4
Step 04 - Genesis 7:11-12

The flood came.

It rained for forty days and forty nights.

5
Step 05 - Genesis 7:16

God protected Noah and his family.

God closed the door and kept Noah's family safe inside.

6
Step 06 - Genesis 8:1-3

The waters receded.

The flood dried up, and the Ark rested on a mountain.

7
Step 07 - Genesis 9:13

God placed a rainbow in the sky.

God put a rainbow in the sky as a sign of His love.

8
Step 08 - Genesis 9:11

God made a promise.

God promised never to flood the Earth again.
